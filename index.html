My Design Process

01

Research

Understanding the problem space through thorough research is my first step. I talk to users, analyze competitors, and identify key opportunities.

02

Ideation

This phase involves brainstorming and sketching multiple solutions. I create wireframes and user flows to test different approaches to the problem.

03

Design

I create high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes. Focus on visual hierarchy, color theory, and usability is paramount in this stage.

04

Testing

Testing with real users helps identify issues and improvement areas. This feedback loop is essential for refining the design.

05

Development

I bring designs to life with clean, semantic code. My development process ensures the final product stays true to the design vision.
